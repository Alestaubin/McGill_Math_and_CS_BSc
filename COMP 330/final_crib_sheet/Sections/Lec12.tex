\subsubsection{Pumping Lemma for CFL's}
For every context-free language $L$, there is an integer $n$, s.t. $\forall z\in L$ of length $>n$,  $\exists z = uvwxy$ s.t. (1)$|vwx| \leq n$, (2)$|vx| > 0$, and (3) $\forall i >0, \; uv^iwx^iy \in L.$
